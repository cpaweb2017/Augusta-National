About Us

Augusta Golf Travel have over ten years’ experience in booking packages for clients travelling to The Masters tournament at the most competitive prices available in the industry. Through years of experience we have built up expert knowledge on providing recommendations based on each client’s requirements to make their trip personable to them.


Our most popular packages being based in Columbia or Athens for 4 or 5 nights including travelling to Augusta to watch one tournament day. Additionally, we are often asked if clients can extend their holiday and travel to different parts of the United States. Here at Augusta Golf Travel we can provide quotations for any package, including add-ons to venues such as Kiawah Island, Sea Pines, Myrtle Beach, Sawgrass or Florida.

We work with over 100 hotels in the area and can provide tickets ranging from practice days to each tournament day, with private transfers or car hire. Hospitality packages can also be arranged. Being ATOL protected and members of the Civil Aviation Authority enables us to offer flights with all major airlines such as British Airways, Delta, American Airlines and Virgin Atlantic where we can arrange upgrades to premium economy or business class.

Financial Protection

We understand how important it is for our client’s monies to be protected. We are members of Protected Trust Services where all client’s monies are held in an independent trust account and we also hold an ATOL license offering customers full financial protection and peace of mind. Our ATOL licence number is 11357 and our PTS Membership number is 5251
